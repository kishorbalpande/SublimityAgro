Supply Chain and Tech

SublimityAgro is India's largest fresh produce supply chain company that is solving one of the toughest problems in the world through technology We connect producers of food directly with retailers, restaurants, and service providers using in-house applications that drive end to end operations.Currently, our Supply Chain is equipped to move 1400 tonnes of perishables from farms to businesses, every day, in less than 12 hours.
